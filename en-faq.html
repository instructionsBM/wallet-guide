2) How to withdraw funds from Bromoney?

To withdraw funds from your Bromoney account, click the Withdraw button on the main page.

There are only two methods:

1) Crypto
Click on the Crypto button. Withdrawals are only available in USDT, on two networks: TON and Bep-20.
Method - select the network.
Address - paste your wallet address.
After that, click the blue Save button at the top right. Enter the amount and click Continue.
2) Money
Input method - select the bank.
Details - Card number or SBP phone number.
Recipient name - the name of the cardholder.
Comment - optional.

3) How to top up your Olimp account from your Bromoney wallet?

You can top up your gaming account from your wallet balance. To do this, log in to Olimp -> top up -> select Bromoney as your payment method -> pay from your wallet balance.

4) Which banks are available for withdrawals?

For KZ users: only Kaspibank by card number.

For the Russian Federation: any bank.

8) Why can't I top up my wallet?

It is not possible to top up within the wallet itself. You can top up your gaming account on the partner's side (Olimp) by selecting the method Transfer from card to Bromoney card.

9) How do I delete my account?

Go to the Bromoney app -> settings in the upper left corner -> Delete account.

10) Why can't I add details that were previously deleted?

Details are unique; you cannot add the same details twice. You can specify the card number of a friend or relative.

11) Why does the balance in my Bromoney wallet change?

The Bromoney balance is tied to the digital $. The amount may differ because the exchange rate may change.
