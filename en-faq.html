Instructions & FAQ

1) How do I login to my Bromoney wallet?

Go to https://m.bromoney.net.

If the link does not open, install the app:

Download for iPhone Download for Android

Select your region, enter your number and the 4-digit code from WhatsApp. Create and remember your password.

2) How to withdraw funds from Bromoney?

Click the Withdraw button on the main page, then click Add details (blue button). Select a method:

1. Crypto: Withdrawals in USDT (TON or Bep-20). Select the network, paste your wallet address, and click "Save".
2. Money: Select your bank, enter card/SBP number, and recipient name.

3) How to top up Olimp from Bromoney?

Log in to Olimp -> Top up -> Select Bromoney -> Pay from your wallet balance.

4) Available banks for withdrawals

  • Kazakhstan: Kaspi Bank (by card number).
  • Russia: Any bank.

5) Minimum withdrawal amount

  • For Kaspi Bank (KZ): 15,300 tenge.
  • For Russia: 2,000 rubles.

6) How long does it take?

From wallet balance: Instantly.

Through an agent: 5 to 75 minutes. Attach a receipt to speed up verification.

7) Why can't I top up my wallet?

Top-ups are not possible inside the wallet. Use the "Transfer to Bromoney card" method on the partner's site (Olimp).

8) How do I delete my account?

App -> Settings (top left corner) -> Delete account.

9) Why does my balance change?

The balance is tied to the digital $ (USDT). The amount may vary slightly due to exchange rate changes.
